# Contact Us

We believe in practicing what we preach. Just as we teach operational security and privacy protection, we apply those same principles to our own communications. That is why we have chosen to remain anonymous and use privacy-respecting tools for contact.

You can never be too safe in today's digital landscape. By maintaining operational anonymity, we protect not only ourselves but also the integrity of this project and the community around it. We chose SimpleX Chat as our primary communication channel because it is a fully open-source platform, both client and server side, that respects your privacy as much as we value our own. SimpleX does not require phone numbers, email addresses, or any personal identifiers. It provides true end-to-end encryption with no metadata collection.
